About

Structural Engineering and Dynamics specialist with combined academic and industrial experience, and recipient of three globally prestigious postdoctoral fellowships: the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA, Europe), the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS, Japan), and the Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (SGES, Switzerland). Research interests cover structural dynamics, vibration control, earthquake engineering, soil–structure interaction and finite element analysis.
