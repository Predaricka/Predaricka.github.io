About

Structural Dynamics specialist with combined academic and industrial experience, and recipient of three globally prestigious postdoctoral fellowships: the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA, Europe), the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS, Japan), and the Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (SGES, Switzerland). Achievements include producing peer-reviewed publications in leading journals and leading international collaborations across Europe and Asia. Research interests cover structural dynamics, vibration control (passive, semi-active, and active), soil–structure interaction, finite element analysis, and the integration of artificial intelligence in vibration engineering.
